AvantiGrout®

Stop leaks. Stabilize soil. Control groundwater. Permanently.

Pomona Pipe Products is pleased to offer you a complete line of Avanti Grouts, Gels, Foams, and other sealants. These grouts improve facilities and operations in mines, dams, sewers, subways, tunnels, tanks, storm drains, basements, parking garages, underground structures, and have been used in numerous other applications including the World Trade Center clean-up after 9/11. From climates as arid as Egypt to the humid locales of Central and South America, our products are used world-wide to stop water infiltration, stabilize soils, encapsulate hazardous waste and control groundwater.

Our grouts have been successfully used for years in probe-grouting, tube-a-manchette grouting, curtain-grouting, the prevention of structural movement due to groundwater, as well as the sealing of entire sewer systems and underground transit systems.

With 24/7 tech support, world renowned animations and the largest collection of educational resources, Avanti continues to lead the industry. Pomona Pipe Products is proud to distribute all Avanti Products.

Inserta Tee

Sewer, Storm, and Manhole Connections

INSERTA TEE is a three piece service connection consisting of a PVC Hub, Rubber Sleeve and special tooling. INSERTA TEEs are designed to connect 2” (51 mm) through 30” (750 mm) laterals to all known solidwall, profile, closed profile, corrugated pipe and manhole structures manufactured today.

INSERTA TEE for NEW & REHAB installations allows:

  • Reduction in labor hours and pipe materials
  • Services to be connected where needed
  • Easier grading of mainline
  • Matching of the internal radius of your pipe or structure for minimal penetration

INSERTA TEE for TAPPING EXISTING LINES without disturbing the bedding while eliminating the need for:

  • Glues, epoxies and grout
  • Tightening and retightening of bands around mainline
  • Awkward Gaskets

When Sliplining, Pipe Bursting and Relining, Inserta Tee allows the following

  • Simple installation with no special equipment required
  • For Pipe Bursting the connection is made directly into solid wall PE and PVC pipe
