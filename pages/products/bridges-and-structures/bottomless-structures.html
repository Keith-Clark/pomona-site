Bottomless Structures

Environmentally-Friendly Solutions.

Bottomless Culverts are an excellent choice where no disturbance to a stream and/or wetland is a requirement of a governing jurisdiction.

  • Where cover over the top of a bottomless culvert is limited to 2' to 4', an aluminum box culvert similar to the one shown on the picture should be specified.
  • Where cover over the top of the bottomless culvert is 5' or greater generally a pure arch would be more cost-effective.

Most jurisdictions do not consider the length of bottomless culverts as a part of the maximum limit for piping a stream.
