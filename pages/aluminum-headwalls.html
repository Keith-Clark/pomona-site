Aluminum Structural Plate

Lightweight. Durable. Strong.

Aluminum Structural Plate offers designers and DOT’s unparalleled durability, design strength and speed of construction. The combination of lightweight material and variety of shapes and sizes also makes this product excellent in reline or rehabilitation applications. When the time it takes to replace or install a new culvert is critical, consider the advantage of an Aluminum Structural Plate structure with Aluminum Structural Plate Headwalls like the one shown in this picture. Pictured to the right: 23'4" x 15'7" Aluminum Structural Plate Pipe Arch with fully welded aluminum structural plate headwalls and internal energy disipating grade control baffles. These culverts are assembled and installed at the job site very quickly with a minimum of disruption to traffic. Pomona Pipe Products, Inc. specializes in Aluminum Structural Plate and Corrugated Aluminum Pipe. Click here for more information about the BENEFITS OF ALUMINUM for a variety of civil engineering applications. Call now for assistance with your next project.

Where the culvert is installed on a steep grade it may be desirable to slow the velocity of the water so that when it discharges from the outlet end, erosion of the stream bed and banks can be minimized. This can be done by installing internal energy disapating grade control baffles.

These structures are custom made to your specifications or requirements.Headwalls can be welded to the ALSP structures or a factory-made pipe.

Benefits of Aluminum Structural Plate:

  • Buried Bridge Structure - Reduces maintenance costs and lowers life cycle costs as there are no bridge deck or joints at the deck/roadway interface.
  • Modular Bridge System - Rapid installation results in reduced overall project costs, delays and detours.
  • Designed “Site Specific” - Meets your site needs in full compliance with AASHTO, ASTM & AREMA design standards for highway and railway use.
  • Easy Site-Adaptability - Flexible layout options to fit within the right-of-way – curve the structure with a stream or adjust for vertical and horizontal clearance requirements.
