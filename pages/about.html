About Pomona

Delivering solutions that bridge the future.

Pomona Pipe Products, Inc. is a locally owned and operated company in the Piedmont Triad area of North Carolina. Our products are manufactured right here in the United States, and we are proud to supply products for a variety of civil engineering projects throughout North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, and Virginia.

Although Pomona Supply Co. was originally formed for the sole purpose of liquidating the inventory of the pipe-products division of Pomona Corporation (a manufacturer of vitrified clay pipe), sales representatives Donald Joyce, Sr. and Tom Glover became "intrigued by the thought of starting a supply company." They thought it would be a great idea to expand the supply company concept and market top-quality building materials for civil engineering projects. In 1981, Joyce and Glover co-founded Pomona Pipe Products, Inc. which quickly became a top distributor of high-quality, corrosion-resistant building products such as Logan® vitirified clay sewer pipe, Superior Clay® flue liners, and Carolina Ceramics® firebrick. Soon after, we began to expand our products to include aluminum drainage pipe and centrifugally-cast fiberglass pipe.

Today, in addition to the aforementioned products, we specialize in the fabrication of aluminum and steel products for a variety of applications. We also offer a complete line of low-pressure valves, sluice gates, repair couplings, and sewer tapping systems. If stopping leaks is a requirement, we carry a complete line of Avanti water-reactive, expandable grouts.
